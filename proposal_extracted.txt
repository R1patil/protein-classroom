Project  Overview  
The  Ved-Structural  Biology  Platform  is  a  first-of-its-kind  educational  tool  designed  to  modernize  medical  education  for  MBBS  and  BAMS  students.  By  leveraging AlphaFold  2 and AlphaFold  3 technology,  the  platform  converts  abstract  biological  sequences  into  interactive  3D  models,  allowing  students  to  visualize  exactly  how  drugs  and  Ayurvedic  
compounds
 
interact
 
with
 
the
 
human
 
body.

●  Medical  Students  (MBBS):  Often  struggle  to  visualize  complex  pharmacological  
interactions,
